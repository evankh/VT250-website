What's been going on these 250 years?

History isn't just a list of things that happened. It's also a narrative we share and tell each other, and which parts we emphasize or forget can tell us a lot about how we see ourselves. We want to know what events and eras stand out the most in our popular vision of America's 250 year history.

The clusters - and the gaps - may provide inspiration for future celebrations and educational opportunities.
