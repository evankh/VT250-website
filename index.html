Welcome to Waterbury VT250!

For the next two years, Waterbury, Vermont is celebrating the 250th anniversaries of the founding of the United States in 1776, and the Vermont Republic in 1777.

This site will have information about upcoming events and celebrations. Each event has its own page under the Events tab, and they're also on the Community Calendar.

The Timeline page shows a timeline of the history of the United States and Vermont, with significant events and eras submitted by community members.

The Blog has in-depth research and educational materials from the Waterbury Historical Society and comunity members.

If you want to contribute your own historical knowledge to either section, contact us!

You can read more about the organizing committee and its goals on the Committee page on the Town of Waterbury's website.

See a list of partnering organizations and participating businesses on the Partners page.
